Mk7 GTI

In 2014, Volkswagen would release their 7th generation of their GTI, the Mk7 GTI. It is a 2.0 liter turbocharged engine with a staggering 220hp and 223 fp of torque. Retail price is $29,999 but you can find used ones for half the price! This car might not sound all that great but with just a few mods you will be on your way to winning races! Not only does it support several mods it comes stock with lots of safety features, so if your not into that and you need a daily driver for you and your family but still want to look good this is the car for you coming with 4drs and 5 seats.
